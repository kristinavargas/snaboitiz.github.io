SN Power is jointly owned by Statkraft and Norfund. It consists of two companies, SN Power AS and Agua Imara AS. SN Power AS focuses exclusively on developments in South East Asia while the business in Africa and Central America is performed through the subsidiary Agua Imara AS. SN Power is dedicated to playing a role as a leading hydropower provider in emerging markets, facilitating development and local economic growth through the delivery of clean, renewable energy.
