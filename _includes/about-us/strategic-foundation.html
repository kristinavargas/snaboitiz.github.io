Strategic Foundation

Mission

Provide electricity, ancillary, and related products and services through world-class technology and operations.

Create shared value with host communities and other stakeholders by operating and developing renewable energy facilities.

Promote a work environment conducive to learning and development, creating opportunities and meaningful careers for team members.

Contribute to the development of the Philippine power industry by implementing and promoting global best practices and corporate governance.

Values

    • Integrity
    • Safety
    • Social Responsibility
    • Innovation
    • Teamwork
    • Excellence

Vision

To be the leading provider and developer of renewable energy preferred by stakeholders, achieving sustained profitable growth for shareholders through a diversified portfolio of assets, products and services delivered by talented and engaged team members.
