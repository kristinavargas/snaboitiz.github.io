New Projects

Our Business Development Program aims to grow our renewable portfolio by looking at potential hydro projects in the Philippines, primarily within its current host communities in northern Luzon.

Alimit Hydro

SNAP is proposing to build the Alimit Hydropower Complex in the province of Ifugao with a potential capacity of 390 MW. A Free Prior and Informed Consent process facilitated by the National Commission on Indigenous Peoples is currently being conducted.
