Sustainability

This framework begins with seven principles upon which the company commits to raise the development value of our community investments, and creating ‘shared value’ with host communities: Partnerships, Purpose, Accountability, Effectiveness, Sustainability, Enabling and Innovation.

ViDA Program

ViDA, or Value in Development Action, is in initiative that openly vests CSR with a developmental purpose and binds the company to act more purposively on creating shared value that links corporate resources and business opportunities with societal needs. It focuses on Partnerships, Policy & Process, and People in terms of committing resources; developing alignment and synergy; and organizational energy.

iWatershed

SNAP’s Watershed Management Project aims to alleviate the rapid degradation of natural resources, including soil degradation. We have also partnered with other organizations on environmental initiatives such as the Aboitiz Passion for Agroforestry and Reforestation to Keep (APARK), McConnell Dowell Reforestation Project and the SNAP-Benguet-NPC Technical Cooperation program. APARK aims to restore forest cover by planting fruit- bearing trees and forest trees within the vicinity of the watershed in cooperation with the local people. MCD Reforestation Project has planted 10,000 seedlings within the watershed. SNAP-Benguet’s technical cooperation with NPC protects areas adjacent to the reservoir through the establishment of firelines and patrolling.
