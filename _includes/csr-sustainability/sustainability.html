Sustainability

This framework begins with seven principles upon which the company commits to raise the development value of our community investments, and creating ‘shared value’ with host communities: Partnerships, Purpose, Accountability, Effectiveness, Sustainability, Enabling and Innovation.

ViDA Program

ViDA, or Value in Development Action, is in initiative that openly vests CSR with a developmental purpose and binds the company to act more purposively on creating shared value that links corporate resources and business opportunities with societal needs. It focuses on Partnerships, Policy & Process, and People in terms of committing resources; developing alignment and synergy; and organizational energy.

iWatershed

SNAP’s Watershed Management Project aims to alleviate the rapid degradation of natural resources, including soil degradation, which decreases the lifespan of the Hydroelectric Power Plant Reservoir Environmental Initiatives were initiated such as Aboitiz Passion for Agroforestry and Reforestation to Keep (APARK), McConnel Dowell Reforestation Project and SNAPBI-NPC Technical cooperation within the 2.5 km radius form the high water mark of the reservoir in Ambuklao-Binga Watershed. APARK aims restore forest cover through planting fruit bearing trees and forest trees within the vicinity of the watershed in cooperation with the local people. MCD Reforestation Project planted 10,000 seedlings within the watershed. SNAPBI-NPC 2.5km Technical Cooperation intend to protect areas adjacent to the reservoir through establishment of firelines and patrolling. Also the Upper Magat Watershed Study will implement its Pilot Implementation Plan (PIP) which aims to reduce soil erosion level in identified areas which has high erosion rates. The PIP will initiate soil erosion control measures and sustainable livelihood project to attain its goal.
